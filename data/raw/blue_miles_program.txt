Airblue Frequent Flyer Program
Airblue offers its loyalty program called BlueMiles, designed to reward frequent flyers for their travel. Membership is FREE, and passengers can easily enroll online. With a BlueMiles membership, you earn miles every time you fly with Airblue. These miles can later be redeemed for flights on both domestic and international routes. Signup Now to take benefits and special offers.

NOTICE:
All earned BlueMiles expire after 36 months(three years) if not used.
A minimum of 8,000 BlueMiles is required before redemption on domestic flight.
Tickets issued on the following basis are no eligible to earn Bluemiles FOC, OCS, Promotional discounts, Barter Agreement, Employee Discount Ticket and Frequent Flyer redemption

Earning Miles
Every time you fly, you can earn miles based on the distance flown and your selected baggage option (Value/Flexi/Xtra). Miles are credited to your account within14 days after flight completion. To ensure your miles are credited in case any are missing:

Provide your BlueMiles membership number when booking from sales offices or send an email along with valid photo ID/PNR/FF account number at helpdesk@airblue.com
If your membership number was not added, you can claim missing miles within 45 days after travel. Members can accrue miles on both domestic and international flights operated by Airblue

Redeeming Miles
Once you accumulate sufficient BlueMiles, you can redeem them for flight tickets on eligible Airblue routes.

Domestic redemption requires 8,000 BlueMiles (One way).
UAE routes requires 12,000 BlueMiles (Oneway).
KSA requires 18,000 BlueMiles (Oneway). Redemptions cover the base fare only—passengers must pay taxes and surcharges separately. All reward tickets are subject to availability
BAKU routes requires 15,000 BlueMiles (One way).

Bonus Miles
Frequent travelers and premium members may receive additional perks or accelerated mile age accumulation based on travel frequency and booking class

Child Passengers
Children with their own BlueMiles membership may earn miles under the same fare conditions as adults.

Miles Rewarded
(Based on Bundle Selection)
Your bundles election during booking determine show many BlueMiles you earn per flight:

Bundle Selection	Miles	Description
Value	50%	Earn half of the total BlueMiles
Flexi	100%	Earn full BlueMiles for the route
Xtra	200%	Earn double BlueMiles for the route

This allows passengers to choose the Baggage type that best suits their travel and rewards preference
