Mobile & Web Check-In
Save time at the airport and enhance your travel experience.

Check-in for your flight from your mobile phone or personal computer.

Save your Boarding Pass to your mobile phone wallet, and speed through the airport.

 

Mobile App Check-In Instructions
CK1
1. View your PNR / E-Ticket
View your PNR / E-Ticket on the Airblue website or mobile app, and click the check-in button.

CK2
2. Review & Agree with Terms
Carefully review the Dangerous Goods questions to continue with check-in.

CK3
3. Click "Check-In" button
Click the Check-In button to confirm your details and receive your boarding pass.

CK4
4. Save your Boarding Pass
Save your Boarding Pass to your mobile phone wallet or print from your computer.
